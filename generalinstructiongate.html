General Instructions

The clock will be set at the server. The countdown timer in the top right corner of the screen will display the remaining time available for you to complete the examination. When the timer reaches zero, the examination will automatically end. You will not be required to end or submit your examination manually.

The Question Palette displayed on the right side of the screen will indicate the status of each question using the following symbols:

  • 1 - You have not visited the question.
  • 3 - You have not answered the question.
  • 4 - You have answered the question.
  • 6 - You have NOT answered the question but have marked it for review.
  • 9 - You have answered the question but marked it for review.

The Marked for Review status indicates that you wish to revisit the question later. If a question is both answered and marked for review, your answer will be considered in the final evaluation.

Navigating to a Question

To answer a question, follow these steps:

  • Click on the question number in the Question Palette to go directly to that question.
  • Click on Save & Next to save your answer and move to the next question.
  • Click on Mark for Review & Next to save your answer, mark it for review, and then proceed to the next question.

Caution: If you move to another question without clicking Save & Next, your answer will not be saved.

To view all questions, click on the Question Paper button. However, answer options for multiple-choice questions will not be displayed.

Answering a Question

Procedure for answering a multiple-choice question:

  • To select your answer, click on the button corresponding to the chosen option.
  • To deselect your answer, click on the same option again or click the Clear Response button.
  • To change your answer, select another option.
  • To save your answer, click Save & Next.
  • To mark the question for review, click Mark for Review & Next.

If an answer is selected for a question marked as Marked for Review, it will be considered for evaluation even if Save & Next was not clicked before submission.

To modify an already answered question, revisit the question and follow the answering procedure.

Negative Marking: For every incorrect answer, **1/3rd of the marks assigned to that question will be deducted**.

No negative marking for unanswered questions.
